Install HTR Radio
Add it to your phone like an app — free, no App Store needed
HTR Radio
15 stations of independent Texas music. Lone Star, Skyline FM, Voltage, Velvet Lounge, Spirit Radio and more.
htr-radio.hellotexasrecords.com
1
Open in Safari
Make sure you're using Safari — not Chrome or another browser. HTR Radio installs as an app through Safari only on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button
Look for the share icon ⬆️ — it's a box with an arrow pointing up. It's usually at the bottom of the screen. If you don't see it, scroll up on the page first to make the toolbar appear.
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
In the share sheet that appears, scroll down past the top row of icons until you see "Add to Home Screen" with a plus icon. Tap it.
4
Name it and tap Add
The name will already say HTR Radio. Tap Add in the top right corner. That's it — the app icon appears on your home screen.
Open HTR Radio in Safari →
Opens full screen. Once installed, HTR Radio opens without any browser bars — just like a real app. No App Store, no sign-up, no cost.
1
Open in Chrome
Open Google Chrome on your Android device and go to htr-radio.hellotexasrecords.com
2
Tap the three-dot menu
Tap the ⋮ three dots in the top right corner of Chrome to open the menu.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Find and tap "Add to Home Screen" in the menu. Chrome may also show an automatic install banner at the bottom — tap that if it appears.
4
Tap Add
Confirm by tapping Add. HTR Radio will appear on your home screen and open full screen just like a native app.
Open HTR Radio in Chrome →
Android may prompt automatically. Chrome sometimes shows an "Add to Home Screen" banner at the bottom of the page on its own — just tap it if you see it.
